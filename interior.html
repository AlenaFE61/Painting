Choosing the right finish for your space

When selecting finishes for your interior project, consider the specific needs and style of each room. High-traffic areas like hallways and kitchens benefit from durable, easy-to-clean finishes like satin or semi-gloss paint. For living rooms and bedrooms, matte or eggshell paints offer a rich, inviting look, while decorative plaster and wallpaper can add unique textures and patterns to enhance the overall aesthetic.
