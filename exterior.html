Exterior painting

Interior painting is more than just a cosmetic upgrade; it’s about transforming spaces to enhance the overall ambiance and functionality. Our interior painting services are designed to deliver high-quality finishes, whether you're looking to refresh a single room or undertake a complete home makeover.

The MBVSLTD team strives to transform your space into a stylish and functional environment that meets your needs and preferences. With over 11 years of combined experience, we guarantee top-notch workmanship and meticulous attention to detail in every project.

From modern minimalism to classic elegance, we work closely with our clients to understand their vision and bring it to life through innovative design solutions and advanced techniques.

Facade painting types

Walls

Walls are exposed to weather conditions, dirt, and mechanical impacts. The choice of paint depends on the wall material (concrete, brick, wood, plaster).

Roofs

Roof paints need to be UV resistant, and durable under temperature fluctuations and humidity.

Windows and Doors

These areas need special attention to ensure longevity, aesthetic appearance, and moisture protection.

Balconies and Terraces

These areas often face water exposure and mechanical loads.

Fences

Fences require paints that can withstand weather conditions and provide long-lasting protection and aesthetic appeal.

Decorative Elements

For decorative elements, aesthetic properties and durability are important.

Benefits of Choosing Paint for Exterior Facades

  • Weather Resistance:

    Exterior paints are designed to withstand harsh weather conditions, including heavy rain, wind, and extreme temperatures, ensuring long-lasting protection for your facade.

  • UV Protection:

    High-quality exterior paints offer excellent UV resistance, preventing the colors from fading and maintaining the vibrancy of your facade over time.

  • Waterproofing:

    Specialized exterior paints provide a waterproof barrier that protects the underlying materials from moisture infiltration and damage.

  • Pest Prevention:

    Certain exterior paints include additives that help deter pests, such as termites and other insects, from damaging the building's structure.

  • Custom Finishes:

    Exterior paints offer a variety of finishes, from matte to gloss, allowing you to achieve the desired look and texture for your facade.

  • Sustainability:

    Eco-friendly exterior paints made with low VOCs and sustainable materials reduce environmental impact and promote a healthier living environment.

  • Enhanced Durability:

    Exterior paints are formulated to be highly durable, providing a tough and resilient coating that protects against physical wear and tear.

  • Improved Insulation:

    Certain exterior paints can improve the insulation of your home, reducing energy costs by maintaining more stable interior temperatures.

Types of Exterior Coatings

Silicone Paint

Highly breathable and water-repellent, ideal for external walls to prevent moisture buildup and enhance durability.

Acrylic Paint

Water-based paint that dries quickly and provides excellent UV resistance, suitable for a wide range of exterior surfaces.

Elastomeric Paint

Provides a flexible, waterproof coating that can bridge hairline cracks and is ideal for masonry, stucco, and concrete surfaces.

Anti-Mold and Insect-Repellent Coating

Formulated to resist mold, mildew, and insect infestations, ensuring a clean, healthy, and protected exterior surface over time.

Metallic Paint

Contains metallic particles that provide a unique look and added protection against corrosion for metal surfaces like fences and gates.

Stone Coating

A thick, textured coating that provides the appearance and durability of stone, suitable for walls, columns, and decorative elements.

Exterior Plaster (Stucco)

Offers a durable and weather-resistant finish, available in various textures and colors, ideal for masonry surfaces.

Polyurethane Coating

Highly durable and resistant to chemicals and abrasion, suitable for protecting concrete and metal surfaces.

By Surface Type:

Mineral Surfaces

Brick, concrete, stone painting.

Use high breathability paints to prevent moisture buildup.


Organic Surfaces

Wood, plywood, fiberboard.

Select highly elastic paints to accommodate material expansion and contraction.


Metal Surfaces

Metal facades, fences.

Use anti-corrosion primers and paints.

By Application Method:

Mechanized Application

Using paint sprayers.

Suitable for large areas, ensures even coverage.


Manual Application

Using brushes, rollers.

Suitable for details and hard-to-reach places.


Combined Application

Combining different methods for optimal results.

By Work Stages:

Preparatory Work

Cleaning surface from dust, dirt, old paint.

Priming the surface.


Main Work

Applying the base coat of paint.

Using multiple layers for even coverage.


Finishing Work

Applying protective coatings (varnishes, water repellents).

Removing masking tape, cleaning the workspace.
