Methods

Air spray gun

The air spray gun is an ideal tool for applying paint quickly and evenly. It uses compressed air to create a fine mist of paint, achieving a smooth and professional finish free from brush or roller marks, particularly useful for working on metal or wooden surfaces.

Paint roller

A paint roller is a practical tool for covering large areas with paint. It allows for quick application with fewer splatters compared to using a brush. Rollers come in various sizes and with different types of nap, making it possible to choose the best tool for the specific type of paint and surface at hand.

Paint brush

The paintbrush is a classic choice for detailed work and achieving precision in hard-to-reach places. Brushes of various shapes and sizes allow the painter to control the application of paint, making them ideal for edges, corners, and other complex details.
